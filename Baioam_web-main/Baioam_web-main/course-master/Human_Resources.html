      Course overview

      Course overview

      This course provides an introduction to the field of human resources management. Topics include job analysis, employee recruitment and selection, training and development, performance appraisal, compensation and benefits, and legal issues related to employment. The course will also examine current trends and challenges in human resources management.

      Human Resource Management

      • Understand the role of human resources management in organizational success.
      • Develop an understanding of the basic principles of job analysis and employee recruitment and selection.
      • Understand the importance of training and development in employee performance and organizational success.
      • Develop an understanding of the principles of performance appraisal and how to use them to improve employee performance.
      • Understand the principles of compensation and benefits and how they are used to attract and retain employees.
      • Understand the legal issues related to employment and the importance of complying with relevant laws and regulations.
      • Develop an understanding of current trends and challenges in human resources management.
